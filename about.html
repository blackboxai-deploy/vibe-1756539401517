About the Conference

The 1st International Conference on Emerging Technologies and Engineering Applications, ICETEA 2026, provides the opportunity for academicians, researchers, practitioners and innovators in various disciplines to advance innovations and emerging fields, such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, the Internet of Things (IoT), Cybersecurity, Blockchain Technologies, 6G Communication, Quantum Computing, and Sustainable Technologies. Although the emphasis is on academia and industry collaboration, the conference will feature emerging research and innovations that support measurable real-world implementations.

The conference promotes education, research, technology and innovation, whilst it seeks to create outputs that can aid in the development of better outcomes for people, and improve security capacity. The conference is in mind with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs):

  • Education → SDG 4: Quality Education
  • Research and Innovation in Health → SDG 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure
  • Technological Advancement → SDGs 9 & 17: Partnerships for the Goals
  • Community and Societal Benefit → SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities and SDG 3: Good Health and Wellbeing
  • Security → SDG 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions

ICETEA 2026 promotes emerging technologies with potential outcomes that meet these global goals in society-based sustainable innovations and advancements, which may materialize into inclusive and secure technologies.

About Silver Oak University

Silver Oak University is a leading educational institution committed to excellence in higher education, research, and innovation. Located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, the university has established itself as a premier destination for quality education in engineering, technology, and applied sciences.

The university is accredited by NAAC and offers state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus environment that fosters learning and research.

About Faculty of Technology

The Faculty of Technology at Silver Oak University is dedicated to providing cutting-edge education and research opportunities in various engineering and technology disciplines. The faculty is equipped with modern laboratories, experienced faculty members, and industry partnerships.

Our programs are designed to meet industry requirements and prepare students for the challenges of the modern technological landscape. The faculty actively engages in research activities and collaborates with industry partners to drive innovation.
