About ICETEA 2026

The 1st International Conference on Emerging Technologies and Engineering Applications (ICETEA 2026) is the first edition of a global forum for such an initiative. ICETEA 2026 is scheduled to take place in hybrid mode on February 19-20, 2026, at the College of Technology, Silver Oak University, Ahmedabad, India.

This conference is aimed at bringing together researchers, academicians, and industry to exchange knowledge in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, IoT, Cybersecurity, Blockchain, 6G Communication, Quantum Computing, and Sustainable Technology.

The papers will be published by Springer (XXXX Series), which has international visibility and impact. The conference topic is consistent with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and discusses aspects of education, innovation, societal, and security benefits.
