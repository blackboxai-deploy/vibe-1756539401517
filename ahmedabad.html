Ahmedabad at a Glance

Discover the vibrant heritage city of Ahmedabad, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that beautifully blends ancient traditions with modern innovation.

UNESCO World Heritage City

Ahmedabad, founded in 1411 AD by Sultan Ahmed Shah, is India's first UNESCO World Heritage City. The city is renowned for its rich Indo-Islamic architecture, vibrant culture, and historical significance.

The walled city of Ahmedabad showcases a unique blend of Hindu and Islamic architectural styles, making it a living heritage that continues to thrive in the modern era.

Must-Visit Attractions

Sabarmati Ashram

The historic residence of Mahatma Gandhi from 1917 to 1930, where he lived and launched the famous Dandi March for India's independence.

Sabarmati, Ahmedabad

Jama Masjid

Built in 1424 AD, this magnificent mosque showcases the finest Indo-Islamic architecture with its beautiful yellow sandstone construction and intricate carvings.

Old City, Ahmedabad

Sidi Saiyyed Mosque

Famous for its exquisite stone lattice work, particularly the "Tree of Life" jali, this 16th-century mosque is an architectural marvel.

Lal Darwaja, Ahmedabad

Adalaj Stepwell

A stunning 15th-century stepwell featuring intricate carvings and architectural brilliance, showcasing ancient water conservation techniques.

Adalaj, Gandhinagar

Kankaria Lake

A beautiful circular lake built in 1451 AD, now a popular recreational spot with gardens, zoo, and various entertainment facilities.

Kankaria, Ahmedabad

Calico Museum of Textiles

One of the world's finest textile museums, showcasing India's rich textile heritage with an impressive collection of fabrics and artifacts.

Shahibaug, Ahmedabad

Culture & Cuisine

Rich Cultural Heritage

  • Traditional Gujarati folk dances and music
  • Vibrant festivals like Navratri and Uttarayan
  • Handicrafts and textile traditions
  • Art galleries and cultural centers

Delicious Cuisine

  • Traditional Gujarati thali
  • Famous street food like dhokla and khandvi
  • Sweet delicacies like mohanthal and ghughra
  • Authentic vegetarian cuisine

Modern Ahmedabad

Business Hub

Major commercial and industrial center of Gujarat with thriving textile and pharmaceutical industries.

Education Center

Home to prestigious institutions like IIM Ahmedabad and numerous engineering colleges.

Innovation Hub

Growing IT sector and startup ecosystem making it a modern technology destination.
